About Me

My name is Grace Wall, and I'm currently a freshman at Rochester Institute of Technology, majoring in medical illustration. I'm planning on entering the medical field from an artistic standpoint. I've been creating art my whole life, and some of it's even won competitions for the Saratoga Museum of Racing, as well as for the benefit of my high school. For the moment, I'm focused on developing my skills further, and to begin sharing my artwork with others.
