'The Race'

This is a painting of a jockey on a horse during a race. I took inspiration from a race that I watched on tv, after I found myself amazed at how physically detrimental the sport can be. The moment I saw was so intense, and I wanted to attempt to capture it myself. I used acrylic paint on a canvas to create this piece. I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed the process.
